Accessibility Statement

Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs is committed to making its websites accessible in accordance with the Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No. 2) Accessibility Regulations 2018.

This accessibility statement applies to get air pollution data

Compliance status

This website is fully compliant with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) version 2.2 AA standard.

Preparation of this accessibility statement

This statement was prepared on 18 September 2024.

The website was evaluated by the Department for Environment, Food & Rural Affairs.

The statement was last reviewed on 16 September 2024.

Feedback and contact information

If you notice any compliance failures or need to request information and content that is not provided in this document, please email accessibility@defra.gov.uk.

Enforcement procedure

The Equality and Human Rights Commission (EHRC) is responsible for enforcing the Public Sector Bodies (Websites and Mobile Applications) (No. 2) Accessibility Regulations 2018 (the ‘accessibility regulations’).

If you’re not happy with how we respond to your complaint, contact the Equality Advisory and Support Service (EASS).
