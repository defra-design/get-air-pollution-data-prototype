Get air pollution data

Use this service to:

  • find air quality monitoring stations
  • download air pollution data
What air pollution data is available?

Air quality monitoring stations collect air pollution data for:

  • nitrogen dioxide (NO2)
  • PM10
  • PM2.5
  • ozone (O3)
  • sulphur dioxide (SO2)

Air pollution data is available in:

  • hourly measurements
  • daily average
  • annual average

You can search for air quality data by:

  • monitoring network
  • pollutant
  • site type
  • location
  • local authority
  • date
What monitoring networks are available?
Automatic Urban and Rural network (AURN)
The AURN provides the highest quality air pollution data in the UK. You can use AURN data to check compliance with UK air quality standards.

Locally-managed automatic monitoring network
Most locally managed automatic monitoring stations are run by local authorities. These monitoring stations provide air pollution data from across the UK, but the data is not used to check compliance with UK air quality standards.
