The AURN is the UK's largest automatic monitoring network. It is the main network used for compliance reporting against the Ambient Air Quality Directive.
This network provides automatic monitoring data from across the UK. It is not part of the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs' national monitoring strategy.

Most of the sites are operated by local authorities. The sites may also include monitoring data from other programmes, industry and airports.
