Birmingham A4540 Roadside

Description and features

Roadside monitoring station beside the A4540 (Watery Lane Middleway) which forms part of Birmingham's ring road. Located at the end of Keeley Street.

The station is on a residential street with roadside trees, near a small car park and a traffic speed camera. Kingston Hill Local Park and Digbeth Branch Canal are within a short distance.
